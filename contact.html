Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you offer home delivery?

A: Yes! We offer home delivery within Chintamani and a 25 km radius. Free delivery is available for orders above ₹500.

Q: How fresh are your products?

A: All our produce is harvested within 24 hours of delivery. We ensure maximum freshness and nutrition.

Q: Are your products certified organic?

A: Yes, our farm is NPOP certified organic. We follow strict organic farming protocols and never use chemical pesticides or fertilizers.

Q: Do you accept bulk orders?

A: Absolutely! We welcome bulk orders for restaurants, hotels, and institutions. Please contact us directly for special pricing and arrangements.

Q: Can I visit your farm?

A: Yes, farm visits are welcome! We encourage people to see our farming practices firsthand. Please call ahead to schedule your visit.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept Cash on Delivery, UPI payments, and bank transfers. Choose your preferred method during checkout.
