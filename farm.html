Our Farming Practices

💧 Water Management & Irrigation

Water conservation is at the heart of our farming operations. We have implemented:

  • Drip Irrigation System: Precision watering that reduces water usage by 40% while improving crop yield and quality
  • Rainwater Harvesting: Collection ponds and storage tanks capture monsoon rainfall for use during dry seasons
  • Mulching: Organic mulch retains soil moisture and reduces evaporation
  • Scheduled Irrigation: Scientific watering based on crop needs and soil moisture levels

🌱 Organic Farming Methods

100% certified organic farm with complete chemical-free practices:

  • Organic Composting: On-farm vermicompost and farmyard manure production from crop residues and animal waste
  • Green Manuring: Growing nitrogen-fixing crops like cowpea and sunhemp to enrich soil naturally
  • Crop Rotation: Systematic rotation prevents soil depletion and breaks pest cycles
  • Companion Planting: Strategic plant combinations for natural pest control and improved growth

🐛 Natural Pest Management

We protect our crops without harmful chemicals:

  • Neem-Based Solutions: Natural pesticides from neem extracts
  • Beneficial Insects: Encouraging ladybugs, lacewings, and other predatory insects
  • Trap Crops: Sacrificial plants that attract pests away from main crops
  • Biopesticides: Microbial and botanical pesticides approved for organic farming
  • Physical Barriers: Netting and row covers for vulnerable crops

🔄 Integrated Farming Systems

Maximizing farm efficiency through integration:

  • Crop Diversity: Growing multiple crops to spread risk and maximize land use
  • Livestock Integration: Small-scale poultry and dairy for additional income and organic manure
  • Agroforestry: Boundary trees provide timber, shade, and windbreak while improving microclimate
  • Aquaculture: Small fish pond for integrated fish-farming (under development)
